About the Service

1. What LifeDesk Is (and Isn't)

LifeDesk provides document preparation and administrative coordination services to help you dispute medical bills and insurance claim decisions. We review your bills and records, identify potential errors, prepare appeal letters, and follow up with insurers on your behalf.

We do NOT provide legal advice. We are not attorneys, and using our service does not create an attorney-client relationship. If you need legal advice, please consult a licensed attorney in your state.

Process

2. How It Works

You upload your medical bill, Explanation of Benefits (EOB), and relevant insurance documents. We analyze them for potential errors — including CPT code unbundling, No Surprises Act violations, and deductible miscalculations. If we identify errors worth disputing, we prepare an appeal letter and submit it to your insurer on your behalf. We monitor the appeal and follow up as needed.

We will notify you of significant developments and will not take actions beyond document preparation and communication with your insurer without your approval.

Authorization

3. Your Authorization

By using LifeDesk, you authorize us to:

  • Review your medical bills, insurance records, and related documents
  • Contact your insurer on your behalf to submit appeals and request information
  • Act as your authorized representative for the limited purpose of disputing the specific bills or claims you've submitted to us

This authorization is limited to the specific dispute(s) you've engaged us for. You can revoke it at any time by emailing hello@getlifedesk.com — we will stop all activity on your behalf within 48 hours.

Pricing

4. Our Fees

We charge 25% of the amount we actually recover for you — and nothing else.

"Recovery" means money returned to you, credits applied to your account, or reductions in your outstanding balance that result directly from our appeal work.

We invoice you only after a successful recovery. Payment is due within 7 days of invoice. If we don't recover anything, you owe us nothing.

We will never charge you an upfront fee, a review fee, or a monthly subscription.

Outcomes

We are good at what we do, and the data on medical billing appeals is encouraging — but we cannot guarantee any particular outcome. Every case is different. Submitting an appeal does not guarantee it will be accepted. We will always give you our honest assessment of whether we think a case is worth pursuing.

Health Data

6. Your Medical Information (HIPAA)

Your medical bills and insurance records contain Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA. When you use LifeDesk, you are authorizing us to handle this information as your authorized representative for the limited purpose of disputing your medical bills.

How we handle your PHI:

  • We encrypt all documents at rest and in transit
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your PHI to any third party
  • The only exception is sharing necessary information with your insurer as part of the appeal process — which is the service you've engaged us for
  • We store documents on Google Drive under a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as required by HIPAA
