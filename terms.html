1 — What LifeDesk is

What we do (and don't do)

LifeDesk provides document preparation and administrative coordination services to help you dispute medical bills and insurance claim decisions. We review your bills and records, identify potential errors, prepare appeal letters, and follow up with insurers on your behalf.

We do NOT provide legal advice. We are not attorneys, and using our service does not create an attorney-client relationship. If you need legal advice, please consult a licensed attorney in your state.

2 — How it works

The process

You upload your medical bill, Explanation of Benefits (EOB), and relevant insurance documents. We analyze them for potential errors — including CPT code unbundling, No Surprises Act violations, and deductible miscalculations.

If we identify errors worth disputing, we prepare an appeal letter and submit it to your insurer on your behalf. We monitor the appeal and follow up as needed.

We will notify you of significant developments and will not take actions beyond document preparation and communication with your insurer without your approval.

How we handle your PHI:
