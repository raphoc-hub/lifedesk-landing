How it works

Three steps. We handle all of them.

From your first upload to cash back in your pocket — we manage every step of the process.

Step 01
Upload your bill

Drop your itemized bill, EOB, and insurance card. PDF, photo, or scan — any format. Takes 30 seconds.

Files are encrypted end-to-end. Only you and your LifeDesk advocate can access them.
Step 02
We find the errors

We check your bill against federal billing rules — unbundling violations, No Surprises Act breaches, deductible miscalculations, duplicate charges.

We review findings with you before doing anything. You approve every step.
Step 03
We fight and collect

We draft the formal appeal letter citing specific federal statutes, send it certified to your insurer, and monitor the case for 30 days.

You only pay 25% when we win. Nothing if we don't.
